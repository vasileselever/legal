# Business Case 1: Digital Case Management System

## Executive Summary
A comprehensive digital case management system to replace manual paper-based processes in Romanian law firms, improving efficiency, reducing errors, and ensuring compliance with Romanian legal standards.

## Problem Statement
Romanian law firms currently face:
- **Manual file management**: Physical documents leading to storage costs and retrieval delays
- **Time inefficiency**: Lawyers spend 30-40% of time on administrative tasks
- **Compliance risks**: Difficulty tracking deadlines per Romanian Civil Procedure Code (NCPC)
- **Collaboration challenges**: Limited real-time collaboration on cases
- **Client dissatisfaction**: Poor visibility into case progress

## Target Market
### Primary Users
- Small to medium law firms (5-50 lawyers) in Romania
- Solo practitioners and boutique firms
- Corporate legal departments

### Market Size
- ~15,000 practicing lawyers in Romania (Union of Romanian Bar Associations)
- ~3,000 law firms nationwide
- Estimated 40% digitalization adoption rate

## Proposed Solution
### Core Features
1. **Digital Case Repository**
   - Centralized storage for all case documents
   - Integration with Romanian judicial portals (Portal.just.ro)
   - Secure cloud storage compliant with GDPR and Romanian data protection laws

2. **Deadline & Calendar Management**
   - Automated tracking of procedural deadlines per NCPC
   - Integration with Romanian court calendars
   - SMS/Email notifications for critical dates

3. **Document Generation**
   - Templates for common Romanian legal documents (contracts, court submissions)
   - Auto-population with case data
   - Electronic signature integration (qualified signatures per eIDAS)

4. **Client Portal**
   - Secure client access to case updates
   - Document sharing and approval workflows
   - Transparent billing and invoicing

5. **Task & Workflow Management**
   - Case assignment and delegation
   - Time tracking for billing
   - Collaboration tools for legal teams

## Technical Requirements
### Compliance
- GDPR compliant data handling
- Romanian data residency requirements
- Lawyer-client privilege protection (Professional Secrecy Law 51/1995)
- Integration with Romanian e-Justice systems

### Technology Stack
- .NET 8 web application (current foundation)
- SQL Server/PostgreSQL for data storage
- Azure/AWS hosting with Romanian data center option
- Mobile apps (iOS/Android) for on-the-go access
